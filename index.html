Chasing Verbs - What do Artists  Rotate?

Searching the MET collection through the Eyes of an Artist.

Richard Serra, the conceptual sculptor, famously said “Drawing is a verb”, created a work on paper called ‘Verblist’,
in which he compiled a list of verbs what he called "actions to relate to oneself, material, place, and process".
Can a search using a verb uncover the processes artists ‘DO’ to make art?
