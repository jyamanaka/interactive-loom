Chasing Verbs - What do Artists  Rotate?

Richard Serra, the conceptual sculptor, famously said “Drawing is a verb”,
and created his famous work on paper called 'Verblist' (1967), in which he compiled a series of verbs
called "actions to relate to oneself, material, place, and process."

Can a search using a verb uncover the processes artists 'DO' to make art?
